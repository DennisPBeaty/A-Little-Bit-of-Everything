The Playlist

My primary playlist, A Little Bit of Everything, is a playlist I created 4 years ago, when I really started to expand my music interests. The playlist currently has 4,406 songs, which comes out to about 11 days worth of listening, and hopefully will entertain you occasionally. Furthermore, the playlist is in chronological order from the date that I added them. The beginning represents my interest in music at the age of 14; the end represents my interest in music at the age of 18.
